How we met

Chris and Stephanie met in 2011 during their time at McMaster University while both studied Math and Stats. Their friendship grew studying and working on assignments together, keeping each other sane with the occasional wacky exam or professor, and co-founding the McMaster Actuarial Society (although, as many of you know, both took a different career path). Things evolved romantically in 2016/2017, and although the years so far have brought various highs and lows, every challenge and success has brought them closer together. They have been lucky to have the support of both of their families and friends as their bond grew, although their most vocal cheerleader still remains their cat “Margie” Margarita, who frequently declares her affection for Chris and is credited with ‘curing’ Chris’ cat allergy. :)
